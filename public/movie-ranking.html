Top Movies of All Time According To Baston

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Mission Impossible

Mission: Impossible is a high-octane action spy thriller movie series that follows the adventures of Ethan Hunt, a top agent of the fictional Impossible Mission Force (IMF), played by Tom Cruise.

The Lord of The Rings

The Lord of the Rings is an epic fantasy adventure movie series based on the novels by J.R.R. Tolkien. Set in the mythical world of Middle-earth, the story follows a young hobbit named Frodo Baggins, who is tasked with destroying a powerful ring that could bring darkness and destruction to the world if it falls into the wrong hands. The series blends elements of fantasy, action, drama, and heroic quests, featuring creatures like elves, dwarves, wizards, orcs, and dark lords.

Galdiator

"Gladiator" is a 2000 epic historical drama directed by Ridley Scott. The film stars Russell Crowe as Maximus Decimus Meridius, a Roman general who is betrayed and forced into slavery. He becomes a gladiator and rises through the ranks of the arena to avenge the murders of his family and his emperor. The movie is renowned for its intense action sequences, compelling storytelling, and powerful performances. It won five Academy Awards, including Best Picture and Best Actor for Crowe.
